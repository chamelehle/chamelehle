Hello Kitty Island Adventure

Hello is sitting on a boat enjoying the rays. When all the sudden a magalodon (a species that is extinct) comes out of nowhere and fires laser beams at her boat. She guides the boat to a nearby island. She crashes into the island and is stranded. What do you do?

1. Follow the beach around the island to see how big it is.
2. Head straight into the forest in front of you to search from fresh water source.
3. Lay on the beach right next to your wrecked boat and catch some more rays.
