Duck down quietly

You duck down and wait very quietly. Hello kitty knows how to stealth from her service in Vietnam. The griffon sniffs a bush, grabs at some berries and then flies away. While kneeling there, you realize you hear running water. You excitedly run towards the source of the noise and find a giant deep pond that is fed by a watefall. It has a nice flat area that would be good for a camp. But it is getting dark and need to think about shelter for the night.

1. Say screw it and sleep in the flat spot next to the pond and try to enjoy a night of sleep on leaves.
2. Head back to the boat to think of a plan.
